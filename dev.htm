About Me 👨‍⚕️

Hello! I'm Mohamed Kholeif 👋 Community Pharmacist, graduated from the Faculty of Pharmacy, Damanhour University with summa cum laude (Graduated with Honours).

I have been working in the field of pharmacy since 2013. I'm interested in technology and AI and how to use them in the field of pharmacy. I currently work in Al-Dawaa Pharmacy, where I combine my pharmaceutical expertise with modern technology to provide the best service to patients.

I learned self-reliance, good management and planning, how to deal and adapt to changes. I'm still learning and growing every day in this amazing field.
